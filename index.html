ABOUT

Olivia Wood is an editor for CYM Media with a knack for eye-catching graphics and rhythmic editing. She is currently finishing her Associate of Arts in Audio Video Media Production at Howard Community College. As a media production student, Olivia has developed a number of skills, from lighting design and camera operation to editing in Adobe Premiere Pro and composing in Ableton Live. She put these skills to work writing, directing, shooting, editing, and composing for her first short film, Repeat. Although she is well-rounded in terms of her production skillset and enjoys working both behind the camera and in an edit suite, her passion lies in post-production. A multi-instrumentalist for over a decade, Olivia edits with a musician’s rhythmic sensibility, bringing a natural pacing to any edit, regardless of whether or not that edit is set to music. And, likewise, as a composer, a pursuit of visual and aural unity informs her work, resulting in compositions that accentuate an edit’s visual rhythm and elevate the narrative at hand. Experienced in volume editing for clients like Pohanka Automotive Group, Olivia brings a cohesive style to all of her edits and can work with precision on a deadline. She is always looking to learn and improve her skillset. Her major career ambitions include editing and scoring a feature-length film and one day starting a production company and directing a full-length feature of her own. Sometimes she daydreams about starting a band on the side.
